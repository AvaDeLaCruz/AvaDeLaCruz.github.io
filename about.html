HI, I'M AVA.

I'm passionate about learning, encouraging diversity in tech through STEM education, and creating projects which help people.

By the end of my sophomore year of high school, I had already exhausted all of the advanced Computer Science classes at my school. During my junior year, I realized I had only two courses left to take in order to graduate: English and Gym. It was then that my love of learning pushed me to apply for college a year early; this way, I could continue to challenge myself academically and learn about what I love: Computer Science. Now, I am a rising sophomore at the University of Southern California pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science.

At USC, I serve as the Director of Service for our Women in Computing organization. I am responsible for organizing and leading Computer Science outreach efforts, such as coding events, throughout the year. Through this role, I am able to combine my love for teaching with my love for Computer Science, all while working to encourage members of communities that are underrepresented in tech to pursue computing-related careers.

This summer, I'm working at Amazon in Seattle, WA as a Software Development Engineer Intern through the Amazon Future Engineer program. I'm currently developing a full-stack web application which will facilitate meeting scheduling between Amazon SDEs and the Amazon API Governance Body, the group which approves code changes that directly affect Amazon.com. I love going to work every day because I know that what I'm building will help many other people by ultimately making it easier for engineers to ship code which improves the shopping experience for our customers.

Feel free to reach out to me, either by LinkedIn or email, about Software Engineering Internships in NYC, Boston, DC, or London for Summer 2020.
